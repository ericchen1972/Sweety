Instructions

Sweety uses an idle computer to operate the LINE desktop app. Through a selected persona, AI keeps consuming a scammer’s time. Please note: AI never contacts scammers first and only replies passively. Editing the persona can make the delay more effective.

“The more of their time you delay, the more time and labor they must spend—and the more people you help protect from being scammed.”

Quick start

  1. Create a contact in the scammer list and enter their LINE name
  2. Choose a persona to use (we recommend one similar to yourself)
  3. Select the contacts Sweety should reply to
  4. Press Start on the dashboard
  5. If you want to take over the conversation yourself, press Stop at any time
  6. Go to sleep

Advanced settings

If the default personas are not precise enough, you can create your own or extend a base persona.

  1. Choose a base persona
  2. Click “Add to custom personas”
  3. Open the custom persona and edit it
  4. Apply the custom persona to a monitored contact
!

Important notes

On macOS, Sweety requires three permissions:

  • Accessibility
  • Screen & System Audio Recording
  • Automation

Keep the LINE desktop app window fully within the left and right edges of the display, or Sweety may fail to recognize it.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What should I do if the other person suspects AI is responding?

Press Stop and take over the conversation yourself. Continue with Sweety after the situation settles.

Can I configure multiple targets at once?

Yes, but keep them within the visible range of the contact list in the main LINE window.

Can Sweety reply to people who are not scammers?

Yes, but it is not recommended.

Why is it called Sweety?
